# Weather-Agent
Gathers information on the temperature in the Grand Canyon
The project collects data from the past 5 years at the Grand Canyon from August 1-14 and prints a 7-day forecast of temperatures for the current year and dates of the trip. I built it to help plan for a trip to the Grand Canyon and to check the past weather patterns to prepare accordingly. The data used is from open-meteo and records the max temps and min temps for that day.
